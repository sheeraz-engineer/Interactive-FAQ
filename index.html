Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to your most common questions

What is this internship about?
This internship helps you learn front-end development using real-world tasks.
What technologies will I use?
You will use HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. No frameworks are required.
Is this internship free?
Yes, this internship is completely free and self-paced.
Will I get a certificate?
Yes, after completing all tasks, you'll receive a verified certificate.
Can beginners join this internship?
Absolutely! It's designed for beginners to intermediate learners.
How do I submit my tasks?
You can submit your task links through the internship portal or Google Form.
Can I add this internship to my resume?
Definitely. It's a great addition to your portfolio and resume.
Where can I ask more questions?
You can contact the support team or use the community discussion forum.
